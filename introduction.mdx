## Introduction

We believe that data engineers can and should do their best work and innovate faster with the right tools. Datangle empowers data-driven organizations and teams to focus more on strategic tasks that deliver innovation and less on operational tasks, by providing a enterprise-grade functionalities and integrates with your existing data stack and tools like Databricks, Snowflake, Google Cloud Query and Amazon Redshift.

Datangle allow data teams to build, test and deploy their data pipelines using a no-code worflow builder and intelligent automation.
